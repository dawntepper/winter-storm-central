Live Weather Radar Map

Track severe weather across the United States with real-time radar data from NOAA. Our interactive weather radar map displays live precipitation, storm movements, and severe weather alerts. Toggle between precipitation radar, satellite infrared, and forecast views.

Real-Time Severe Weather Alerts

Monitor active weather warnings, watches, and advisories from the National Weather Service. StormTracking tracks winter storms, hurricanes, tornadoes, flooding, extreme heat, and wildfire weather across all 50 states with alerts updated every 30 minutes.
